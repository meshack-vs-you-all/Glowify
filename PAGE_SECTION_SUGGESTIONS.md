## Recommended Additional Pages

### 1. **FAQ/Help Center Page** (High Priority)
**Why:** Reduces support inquiries, builds trust, improves SEO
- Common questions about products, shipping, returns
- Searchable FAQ section
- Accordion-style layout for easy navigation
- Categories: Shipping, Returns, Products, Account, Payment

### 2. **Shipping & Returns Policy Page** (High Priority)
**Why:** Legal requirement, builds customer confidence
- Clear shipping timelines and costs
- Return/exchange policy
- International shipping information
- Processing times

### 3. **Privacy Policy Page** (Required)
**Why:** Legal requirement (GDPR, CCPA compliance)
- Data collection practices
- Cookie policy
- User rights
- Contact information for privacy concerns

### 4. **Terms of Service Page** (Required)
**Why:** Legal protection, sets expectations
- Terms of use
- User responsibilities
- Intellectual property
- Limitation of liability

### 5. **Size Guide Page** (High Priority for Baby Products)
**Why:** Reduces returns, improves customer satisfaction
- Size charts for different product categories
- Measurement instructions
- Age/weight recommendations
- Visual guides with images

### 6. **Blog/News Page** (Medium Priority)
**Why:** SEO benefits, content marketing, customer engagement
- Product care tips
- Parenting advice
- Product spotlights
- Company updates
- SEO-friendly structure

### 7. **Gift Guide Page** (Medium Priority)
**Why:** Increases average order value, helps customers
- Curated gift collections by occasion
- Age-appropriate suggestions
- Price range filters
- Quick add-to-cart functionality

### 8. **Wishlist/Favorites Page** (Medium Priority)
**Why:** Increases conversion, improves UX
- Save products for later
- Share wishlist functionality
- Email reminders
- Quick add to cart

### 9. **Store Locator Page** (Low Priority - if applicable)
**Why:** If you have physical locations
- Map integration
- Store hours
- Contact information
- Directions

### 10. **Care Instructions Page** (Medium Priority)
**Why:** Reduces product damage, improves satisfaction
- Washing instructions
- Material care guides
- Storage recommendations
- Product-specific care tips

## Design Considerations

- **Consistency:** All new pages should follow the existing design system
- **Mobile-First:** Ensure all new sections are fully responsive
- **Accessibility:** Maintain WCAG AA compliance
- **Performance:** Optimize images and lazy-load content
- **SEO:** Include proper meta tags, structured data, and semantic HTML

---

## Notes

- Consider using Shopify's built-in page templates for simple pages
- Use sections for flexible, reusable content blocks
- Ensure all new pages are added to navigation menus
- Test on multiple devices and screen sizes
- Consider A/B testing for high-impact sections like testimonials
